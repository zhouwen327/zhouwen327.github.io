* Note: Introduction to Computer is the first professional compulsory course for computer and related majors, and the leading course for many follow-up courses. It is built on the basis of the cognitive model of computing discipline, with the cultivation of computing thinking ability as the core, and guides the computing discipline from the perspective of discipline ideas and methods. The course focuses not only on the introduction of computer science, but also on the cultivation of students' basic computer operation ability
